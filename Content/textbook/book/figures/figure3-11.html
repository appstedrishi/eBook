Figure 3.11 Quaternary Structure of a Protein

Hemoglobin consists of four folded polypeptide subunits that assemble themselves into the quaternary structure represented by the ribbon model (A) and spacefilling model (B). In both graphic representations, each type of subunit is a different color (α subunits are blue and β subunits are green). The heme groups (red) contain iron and are the oxygen-carrying sites.

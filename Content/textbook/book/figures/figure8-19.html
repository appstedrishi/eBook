Figure 8.19 pH Affects Enzyme Activity

An enzyme catalyzes its reaction at a maximum rate. The activity curve for each enzyme peaks at its optimal pH. For example, pepsin is active in the acidic environment of the stomach, whereas chymotrypsin is active in the small intestine.

Steps of light microscopy

  1. light is moved through a specimen
  2. light is moved through a glass lens
  3. light is refracted by a glass lens
  4. Create produces an image
  5. An image is expanded by a glass lens
  6. light is Sensed by an eye in an image resulting in a message

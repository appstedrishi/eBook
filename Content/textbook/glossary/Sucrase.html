Functions of sucrase

  • Hydrolysis of sucrose — sucrose and a water molecule are converted to fructose and glucose at an active site. This process transforms activation energy to free-energy.

Sucrase also does…

  • Hydrolysis of sucrose — activation energy, sucrose and a water molecule are converted to free-energy, fructose and glucose at an active site. Here, sucrase is a catalyst.

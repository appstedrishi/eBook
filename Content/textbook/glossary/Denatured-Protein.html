denatured protein

A biolgically inactive protein that has lost its functional conformation by exposure to a destabilizing agent such as heat or detergent.

Definition of a denatured protein

A biolgically inactive protein that has lost its functional conformation by exposure to a destabilizing agent such as heat or detergent.

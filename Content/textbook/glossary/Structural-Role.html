Definition of a structural role

This is the functional role played by organisms due to its structural shape.

Structural role is a type of: role.

Examples of a structural role

  • The function of aster is support of a cell by an aster with a microtubule. Here, the aster is a structural role.
  • A cell is supported by a structural polysaccharide. Here, the structural polysaccharide is a structural role.
  • The function of cytoskeleton is support of a cell by a cytoskeleton with a microtubule. Here, the cytoskeleton is a structural role.
  • A cell is supported by chitin. Here, the chitin is a structural role.
  • A cell is supported by cellulose. Here, the cellulose is a structural role.

formation of cell plate

Definition of a formation of cell plate

During mitosis in plant cells, the process of forming a new cell wall, which results in separation of the two daughter cells.

Formation of cell plate is a type of: mitotic event.

Steps of formation of cell plate

  1. 2 vesicles are produced in Golgi apparatus
  2. 2 vesicles are brought together at a center through a microtubule
  3. A vesicle is fused to another vesicle

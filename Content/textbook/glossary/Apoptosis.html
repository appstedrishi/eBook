apoptosis

Definition of an apoptosis

Steps of apoptosis

  1. Hydrolysis — a DNA and a water molecule are converted in a eukaryotic cell to a chemical and an organic molecule. This process transforms activation energy to free-energy.
  2. Hydrolysis — protein and a water molecule are converted in a cell to 2 chemicals at an active site. This process transforms activation energy to free-energy.
  3. cellular structure is destroyed by a hydrolase
  4. An organelle is destroyed by a hydrolase in a cell
  5. Contract in a cell
  6. Blebbing in a cell resulting in another cell
  7. A vesicle is produced in a cell using a biomembrane and cellular structure
  8. Chemical digestion of an object inside a food vacuole by a lysosomal enzyme in a eukaryotic cell

Anchorage dependence

Anchorage dependence

Mechanism of regulation of cell division, in which contact with a substrate (such as a culture vial or tissue) is required in order for a cell to divide. Anchorage dependence prevents free-floating or isolated cells from dividing.

Definition of an Anchorage dependence

Transpirational pull

transpirational pull

Transpirational pull is the pull exerted on xylem sap due to transpiration. This pull is responsible for the upward movement of xylem sap from root towards leaves.

Definition of a transpirational pull

Transpirational pull is the pull exerted on xylem sap due to transpiration. This pull is responsible for the upward movement of xylem sap from root towards leaves.

Transpirational pull is a type of: plant process.

Steps of transpirational pull

  1. water is pulled by an air space with a force from an air water interface
  2. water is pulled by a mesophyll cell with a force from a plant cell

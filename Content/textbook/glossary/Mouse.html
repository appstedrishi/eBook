mouse

Common name for a small mammal member of the class rodentia, characterized by elongated front teeth adapted for gnawing and a long, hairless tail.

Definition of a mouse

Common name for a small mammal member of the class rodentia, characterized by elongated front teeth adapted for gnawing and a long, hairless tail.

Mouse is a type of: mammal.

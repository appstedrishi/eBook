Steps of translocation during translation

  1. A GTP is broken resulting in a GDP and a phosphate ion
  2. An MRNA and a TRNA are brought together at a P-Site
  3. aminoacyl-tRNA is moved in a ribosome from cytoplasm
  4. A TRNA is moved to an E-Site from a P-Site
  5. Exit in a ribosome to cytoplasm from an E-Site through a P-Site

Locomotion of eukaryotic cell by flagellum

locomotion of eukaryotic cell by flagellum

Locomotion, usually swimming, of a eukaryotic cell by means of a beating flagellum. Most animals have sperm that use flagella for locomotion.

Definition of a locomotion of eukaryotic cell by flagellum

Locomotion, usually swimming, of a eukaryotic cell by means of a beating flagellum. Most animals have sperm that use flagella for locomotion.

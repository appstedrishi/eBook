Definition of a horizontal transmission

When a plant is infected from an external source of the virus.

Participants of a horizontal transmission

A plant virus is transmitted by a virus in a plant with an insect

Steps of horizontal transmission

  1. A genome is moved by a virus through a plasma membrane
  2. ATP, an amino acid, a protein enzyme and a TRNA are taken control of by a genome and a virus
  3. A capsid and a genome are joined resulting in a virus
  4. A virus leaves away from a cell

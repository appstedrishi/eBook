Participants of a dephosphorylation

Dephosphorylation consumes an organic molecule and produces an anion and a chemical. This process requires energy.

  • inactivation of transport protein
  • inactivation of relay molecule
  • inactivation of phosphorylated kinase
  • dephosphorylation of glucose 6-phosphate
  • dephosphorylation of ATP

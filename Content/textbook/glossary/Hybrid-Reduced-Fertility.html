hybrid reduced fertility

A post-zygotic barrier in which two species mate and produce hybrid offspring that are vigorous, but the hybrids are completely or largely sterile.

Definition of a hybrid reduced fertility

A post-zygotic barrier in which two species mate and produce hybrid offspring that are vigorous, but the hybrids are completely or largely sterile.

Hybrid reduced fertility is a type of: reproductive isolation .

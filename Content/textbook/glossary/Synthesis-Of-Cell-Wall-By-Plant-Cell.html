synthesis of cell wall by plant cell

Definition of a synthesis of cell wall by plant cell

The process by which plant cells produce cell walls, during which vesicles of carbohydrates produced by the Golgi apparatus are delivered to the outside of the cell.

Synthesis of cell wall by plant cell is a type of: anabolism.

Properties of a synthesis of cell wall by plant cell

  • Free energy change
    • inherent to all endergonic processes
      • positive with respect to a spontaneous process

Participants of a synthesis of cell wall by plant cell

A molecule and a plant cell-wall are synthesized by a plant cell in an organism using another molecule. This process requires free-energy.

Steps of synthesis of cell wall by plant cell

  • Transport of an organic molecule in a cell to an extracellular side with a transport vesicle from cytoplasm through a plasma membrane using another organic molecule. This process requires chemical energy and free-energy.

polyadenylation

The addition of adenine nucleotides to mRNA. The poly-A tail (along with the 5'cap) functions to facilitate the export of the mature mRNA from the nucleus.

Definition of a polyadenylation

The addition of adenine nucleotides to mRNA. The poly-A tail (along with the 5'cap) functions to facilitate the export of the mature mRNA from the nucleus.

Polyadenylation is a type of: biomolecular process .

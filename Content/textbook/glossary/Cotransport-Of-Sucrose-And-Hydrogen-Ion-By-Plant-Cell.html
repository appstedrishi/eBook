Definition of a cotransport of sucrose and hydrogen ion by plant cell

The simultaneous movement of sucrose and hydrogen ions across a membrane in opposite directions.

Cotransport of sucrose and hydrogen ion by plant cell is a type of: uptake of nutrient by plant cell using proton pump .

Participants of a cotransport of sucrose and hydrogen ion by plant cell

free-energy, a hydrogen ion, a hydrophilic compound and sucrose are transported by a sucrose hydrogen ion cotransporter in a plant cell through a biomembrane

Steps of cotransport of sucrose and hydrogen ion by plant cell

  • Active transport using ATP
  • Exergonic process, which is facilitated diffusion using carrier protein

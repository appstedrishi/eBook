Homeodomain

homeodomain

A highly conserved protein sequence motif. It comprises 60 amino acids and contains a DNA-binding helix–turn–helix motif indicating that acts as a transcription factor. The homeodomain is encoded for by a homeobox.

Definition of a homeodomain

A highly conserved protein sequence motif. It comprises 60 amino acids and contains a DNA-binding helix–turn–helix motif indicating that acts as a transcription factor. The homeodomain is encoded for by a homeobox.

Homeodomain is a type of: protein domain.

Unsaturated fatty acid

unsaturated fatty acid

A fatty acid possessing one or more double bonds between the carbons in the hydrocarbon chain. Such bonding reduces the number of hydrogen atoms attached to the carbon skeleton.

Definition of an unsaturated fatty acid

A fatty acid possessing one or more double bonds between the carbons in the hydrocarbon chain. Such bonding reduces the number of hydrogen atoms attached to the carbon skeleton.

Unsaturated fatty acid is a type of: fatty acid.

Participants of a eukaryotic gene expression

A gene is expressed by a transcription factor in a eukaryotic cell resulting in protein

Steps of eukaryotic gene expression

  • Synthesis of mRNA — an MRNA and a water molecule are synthesized in a cell using ATP and 2 monomers. This process transforms activation energy and free-energy to another free-energy.
  • Synthesis of protein — 2 monomers are converted in a cell to protein and a water molecule. This process transforms activation energy and free-energy to another free-energy.

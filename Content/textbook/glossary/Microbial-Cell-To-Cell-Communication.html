Definition of a microbial cell to cell communication

The secretion of small molecules whose increase in concentration, when detected by other bacterial cells allows them to monitor the local density of cells allowing bacterial populations to coordinate their behaviors so they can carry out activities that are only productive when performed by a given number of cells in synchrony.

Microbial cell to cell communication is a type of: chemical signaling .

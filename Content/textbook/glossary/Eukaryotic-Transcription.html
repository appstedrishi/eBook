Participants of a eukaryotic transcription

Transcription by a gene by a transcription factor in a cell and a transcription unit to a 3 prime end from a 5 prime end using 2 ATP and a monomer resulting in an RNA and a water molecule in a nucleus. This process transforms activation energy and free-energy to another free-energy.

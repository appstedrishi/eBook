Dynein walking

dynein walking

The process of dynein arms of one doublet attaching to an adjacent doublet and pulling so that the doublets slide past each other in opposite directions. Responsible for the bending movements of cilia and flagella.

Definition of a dynein walking

The process of dynein arms of one doublet attaching to an adjacent doublet and pulling so that the doublets slide past each other in opposite directions. Responsible for the bending movements of cilia and flagella.

Dynein walking is a type of: biomolecular process .

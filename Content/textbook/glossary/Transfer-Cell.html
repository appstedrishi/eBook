Transfer cell

transfer cell

In a plant, a companion cell with numerous ingrowths of its wall, which increase the cell's surface area and enhance the transfer of solutes between apoplast and symplast.

Definition of a transfer cell

In a plant, a companion cell with numerous ingrowths of its wall, which increase the cell's surface area and enhance the transfer of solutes between apoplast and symplast.

Transfer cell is a type of: companion cell.

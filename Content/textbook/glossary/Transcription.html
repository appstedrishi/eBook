Participants of a transcription

Synthesis of an RNA and a water molecule by a transcription factor in a cell and a transcription unit toward a 3 prime end using a gene from a 5 prime end using ATP and a monomer in an active site. This process transforms activation energy and free-energy to another free-energy.

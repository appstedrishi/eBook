Definition of a chromosomal translocation

An aberration in chromosome structure resulting from attachment of a chromosomal fragment to a nonhomologous chromosome.

Participants of a chromosomal translocation

Steps of chromosomal translocation

  1. A chromosome is broken resulting in a chromosome fragment
  2. A chromosome fragment is attached to a non homologous chromosome

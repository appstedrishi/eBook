Definition of a phosphoglyceromutase

The enzyme phosphoglyceromutase relocates the Phosphate group of 3-phosphoglyceric acid from the third carbon to the second carbon to form 2-phosphoglyceric acid.

Phosphoglyceromutase is a type of: isomerase.

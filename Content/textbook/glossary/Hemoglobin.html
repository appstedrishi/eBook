Hemoglobin

hemoglobin

An iron-containing protein in red blood cells that reversibly binds oxygen.

Definition of a hemoglobin

An iron-containing protein in red blood cells that reversibly binds oxygen.

Hemoglobin is a type of: respiratory pigment and globular protein.

electron carrier of photosynthesis

A coenzyme, NADP+, that accepts electrons from the electron transport chain in photosynthesis, then gives them up in the Calvin Cycle, in order to make sugar.

Definition of an electron carrier of photosynthesis

A coenzyme, NADP+, that accepts electrons from the electron transport chain in photosynthesis, then gives them up in the Calvin Cycle, in order to make sugar.

Electron carrier of photosynthesis is a type of: electron carrier.

Some functions of different types of protein enzymes

  • Ligase
    • aminoacyl-tRNA is created using a chemical in an active site
    • Recombinant DNA technology by a DNA ligase
    • A DNA piece is attached by a DNA ligase to another DNA piece
  • Hydrolase
    • lysozyme is attached to a cell wall at an active site
    • In cell communication leading to apoptosis In caenorhabditis elegans, a hydrolase is activated by a ced-3
    • Chemical reaction — cellulose and a water molecule are converted in a cell for an organism to glucose. This process requires activation energy and potential energy.
    • In webbed foot development, a DNA is destroyed by nuclease in a eukaryotic cell
    • foreign DNA is cut by a restriction nuclease using a water molecule resulting in at least 2 sticky ends at restriction site
    • In staggered cut, restriction site is recognized by a restriction nuclease
  • Transferase
    • In cell signaling with plasma membrane receptor, signal reception — a cell receives a signal at a receptor tyrosine-kinase carried by a chemical
    • A transposon is moved by a transposase to a locus from another locus
  • Herceptin
    • A HER2 is blocked by a herceptin

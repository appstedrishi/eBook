companion cell

Functions of a companion cell

  • Eukaryotic cellular respiration — glucose, 2 NAD plus and 6 oxygen molecules are converted in a companion cell to 32 ATP, 6 carbon dioxide, 2 NADHs and 6 water molecules in cytoplasm. This process transforms activation energy and potential energy to chemical energy, free-energy and thermal energy.

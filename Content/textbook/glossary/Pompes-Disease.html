Pompes disease

pompes disease

Pompe disease is a neuromuscular, autosomal recessive metabolic disorder in the family of lysosomal storage diseases caused by a deficiency in the enzyme Acid alpha-glucosidase which is needed to break down glycogen for energy. The lysosomes become engorged with indigestible substrates, that interfere in cellular activities

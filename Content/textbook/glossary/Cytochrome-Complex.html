Definition of a cytochrome complex

A cytochrome complex also does…

  • In redox reaction during cyclic electron flow, an electron transfers from a cytochrome complex to plastocyanin. Here, the cytochrome complex is a reducing agent.
  • In redox reaction during cyclic electron flow, an electron transfers from ferredoxin to a cytochrome complex. Here, the cytochrome complex is an electron recipient.

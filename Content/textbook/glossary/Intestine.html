Definition of an intestine

Segment of the alimentary canal which participates in the absorption of nutrients from food. The intestine can be subdivided into two main segments, the small intestine and the large intestine.

Intestine is a type of: organ.

Figure 5.16
Microfilaments for Support Cells that line the intestine are folded into tiny projections called microvilli, which are supported by microfilaments. The microfilaments interact with intermediate filaments at the base of each microvillus. Microvilli increase the surface area of a cell, facilitating its absorption of small molecules.

Definition of a spider

Members of the order Araneae. They are air-breathing arthropods that have eight legs, and chelicerae with fangs that inject venom. They are the largest order of arachnids and rank seventh in total species diversity among all other groups of organisms.

Spider is a type of: arachnid.

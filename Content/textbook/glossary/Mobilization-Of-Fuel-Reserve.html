Mobilization of fuel reserve

mobilization of fuel reserve

The process in animals in which stored chemical energy, such as that in glycogen or fat, is utilized. This process is usually associated with the fight or flight response.

Definition of a mobilization of fuel reserve

The process in animals in which stored chemical energy, such as that in glycogen or fat, is utilized. This process is usually associated with the fight or flight response.

Mobilization of fuel reserve is a type of: macrophysiological process .

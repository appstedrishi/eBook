A fructose-6-phosphate, which is an organic molecule also does…

  • ATP and a fructose-6-phosphate are converted by phosphofructokinase to a fructose 1, 6 bisphosphate in an active site. Here, the fructose-6-phosphate is a reactant.
  • In energy investment phase of glycolysis, ATP and a fructose-6-phosphate are converted by phosphofructokinase to an ADP and a fructose 1, 6 bisphosphate in an active site. Here, the fructose-6-phosphate is a reactant.

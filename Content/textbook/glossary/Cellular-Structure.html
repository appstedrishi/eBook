  • Nuclear lamina

  • Microvillus
    • In small intestine, increase by a microvillus
  • Vesicle
    • In receptor mediated endocytosis, a chemical is carried by a coated vesicle
  • Ribosome
    • Synthesis of protein — a monomer and polypeptide are converted in a cell to a secretory protein and a water molecule at a bound ribosome. This process transforms activation energy and free-energy to another free-energy.
  • Microtubule organizing center
    • A microtubule is organized by a microtubule organizing center
    • A microtubule is produced by a microtubule organizing center using tubulin

    • In animate thing, debris is moved by a motile cilium out of an object through a biological region
    • A prokaryote is moved with a prokaryotic cilium

    • In meiotic event, 2 cellular structures are separated by a kinetochore microtubule from a metaphase plate

Definition of a cell fractionation

The disruption of a cell and separation of its parts by centrifugation.

Participants of a cell fractionation

Cell fractionation — an organelle and a protein enzyme are fractionated in a cell

Steps of cell fractionation

  1. Homogenization — a cell is homogenized resulting in a homogenate
  2. Centrifugation with a centrifuge

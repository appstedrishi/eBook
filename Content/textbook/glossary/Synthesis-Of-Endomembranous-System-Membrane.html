Participants of a synthesis of endomembranous system membrane

Synthesis of endomembranous system membrane — a biomembrane is synthesized in a eukaryotic cell. This process requires free-energy.

Steps of synthesis of endomembranous system membrane

  • A transport vesicle is added to a plasma membrane
  • A transport vesicle is removed from Golgi apparatus
  • A plasma membrane is reached by a transport vesicle
  • A carbohydrate is attached to lipid at Golgi apparatus
  • A transport vesicle is fused to Golgi apparatus
  • A transport vesicle is released by a smooth endoplasmic reticulum
  • Synthesis of glycoprotein — a bound ribosome converts 2 monomers in a eukaryotic cell to a glycoprotein and a water molecule. This process transforms activation energy and free-energy to another free-energy.
  • Synthesis of lipid — a glycolipid is synthesized in a eukaryotic cell using a chemical in a smooth endoplasmic reticulum. This process requires free-energy.

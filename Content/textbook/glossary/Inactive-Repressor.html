Inactive repressor

inactive repressor

A protein that inhibits gene transcription. In prokaryotes, repressors bind to the DNA in or near the promotor. In eukaryotes, repressors may bind to control elements within enhancers, to activators, or to other proteins in a way that blocks activators from binding to DNA.

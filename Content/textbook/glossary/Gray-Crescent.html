Gray crescent

gray crescent

A light gray, crescent-shaped region of cytoplasm that becomes exposed after cortical rotation, located near the equator of an egg on the side opposite sperm entry, marking the future dorsal side of the embryo.

Definition of a gray crescent

A light gray, crescent-shaped region of cytoplasm that becomes exposed after cortical rotation, located near the equator of an egg on the side opposite sperm entry, marking the future dorsal side of the embryo.

Gray crescent is a type of: embryonic region.

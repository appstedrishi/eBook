Participants of a translation elongation

Translation elongation — an amino acid and a GTP are converted in a cell using a TRNA resulting in polypeptide at a ribosome. This process transforms free-energy to thermal energy.

Steps of translation elongation

  1. Codon recognition — aminoacyl-tRNA recognizes in an RNA codon in a ribosome
  2. Peptide bond formation — a peptide bond forms by ribosomal RNA and a ribosome
  3. Translocation during translation — a TRNA is translocationed during translation in a ribosome

Functions of a perennial

  • starch is stored by a tomato plant in another tomato plant in a lateral root
  • Crassulacean acid metabolism — a lithops performs crassulacean acid metabolism in a mesophyll cell using carbon dioxide and a water molecule resulting in glucose and an oxygen molecule in an active site, a chloroplast and cytoplasm. This process transforms free-energy and visible light to chemical energy.

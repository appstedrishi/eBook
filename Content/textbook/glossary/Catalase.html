Functions of a peroxidase

  • Synthesis reaction consumes an organic molecule and an oxygen molecule and produces a hydrogen peroxide
  • A fatty acid is converted by a peroxidase to a carbohydrate and a chemical in a glyoxysome. This process requires energy.

A peroxidase also does…

  • The function of catalase is conversion of a chemical to another chemical in an active site. Here, a peroxidase is a catalyst.

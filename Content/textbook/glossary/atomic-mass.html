Atomic mass

atomic mass

Atomic mass is the sum of the masses of the protons, neutrons and electrons in a specific isotope of an atom, measured in daltons. Because the mass of an electron is very small and does not contribute much to the overall mass of an atom, the atomic mass number is a close approximation of the actual mass of an atom.

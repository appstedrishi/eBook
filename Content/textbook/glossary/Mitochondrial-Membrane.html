Mitochondrial membrane

mitochondrial membrane

Double lipid bilayer which surrounds the mitochondrion and forms the infolds, called cristae, which function in the reactions of cellular respiration.

Definition of a mitochondrial membrane

Double lipid bilayer which surrounds the mitochondrion and forms the infolds, called cristae, which function in the reactions of cellular respiration.

Mitochondrial membrane is a type of: double membrane .

Properties of a mitochondrial membrane

  • Fluidity
    • The fluidity of the mitochondrial membrane is directly proportional to the rate of move.
  • Density
    • The denseness of the mitochondrial membrane is inversely proportional to the fluidity of the mitochondrial membrane.
  • Temperature
    • The temperature of the mitochondrial membrane is directly proportional to the quantity of the kinetic energy and the fluidity of the mitochondrial membrane.

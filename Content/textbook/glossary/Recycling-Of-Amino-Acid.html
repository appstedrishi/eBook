Recycling of amino acid

recycling of amino acid

The break down of proteins via hydrolysis to form amino acids. These amino acids will then be used for protein synthesis or metabolism as in glycolysis or the citric acid cycle. Recycling of Amino Acids is an important process when the cell is starved of amino acids from an outside source. It cannibalizes its own proteins to provide itself with amino acids.

Steps of recycling of amino acid

  • Hydrolysis consumes protein and a water molecule and produces an amino acid and a chemical. This process transforms activation energy to free-energy.
  • Synthesis of protein — 2 monomers are converted in a cell to protein and a water molecule. This process transforms activation energy and free-energy to another free-energy.

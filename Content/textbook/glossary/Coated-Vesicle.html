coated vesicle

Coated-vesicle is formed as a result of intake of a receptor bound molecule by the process of pinocytosis. It bears the receptor bound molecule on its surface.

Definition of a coated vesicle

Coated-vesicle is formed as a result of intake of a receptor bound molecule by the process of pinocytosis. It bears the receptor bound molecule on its surface.

Coated vesicle is a type of: vesicle.

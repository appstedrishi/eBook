Participants of a selective degradation

Selective degradation — a proteasome and a ubiquitin degrade selectively using protein and a water molecule resulting in polypeptide. This process releases free-energy.

Steps of selective degradation

  1. A ubiquitin is attached to protein
  2. protein is recognized by a ubiquitin
  3. Hydrolysis — a proteasome converts protein and a water molecule to polypeptide. This process transforms activation energy to free-energy.

Definition of a telophase

The fifth and final stage of mitosis, in which daughter nuclei are forming and cytokinesis has typically begun.

Telophase is a type of: mitotic stage.

Participants of a telophase

Telophase in a cell resulting in 2 nuclei

Steps of telophase

  1. A nuclear membrane is produced in a cell using a biomembrane
  2. A nucleus is created in a eukaryotic cell
  3. A microtubule is disassembled in a eukaryotic cell resulting in tubulin
  4. A mitotic spindle is disassembled in a eukaryotic cell

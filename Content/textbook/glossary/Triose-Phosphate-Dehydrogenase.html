Triose phosphate dehydrogenase

triose phosphate dehydrogenase

The enzyme that catalyzes the sixth step of glycolysis where, first, a hydrogen from glyceraldehyde-3-phosphate is transferred to NAD+ to form NADH, and second, it adds a phosphate from the cytosol to the oxidized glyceraldehyde-3-phosphate to form 1,3-bisphosphoglycerate.

Definition of a triose phosphate dehydrogenase

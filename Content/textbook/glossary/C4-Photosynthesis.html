Definition of a C4-photosynthesis

The C4 photosynthesis is called C4 because the CO2 is first incorporated into a 4-carbon compound. PEP Carboxylase is the enzyme involved in the uptake of CO2.

C4-photosynthesis is a type of: adaptation and photosynthesis.

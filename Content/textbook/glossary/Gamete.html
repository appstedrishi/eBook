Definition of a gamete

A haploid reproductive cell, such as an egg or sperm. Gametes unite during sexual reproduction to produce a diploid zygote.

Gamete is a type of: haploid cell and reproductive cell .

A gamete also does…

  • In fungal life cycle, meiosis — a diploid cell undergoes meiosis using ATP resulting in a gamete. Here, the gamete is a daughter cell.
  • In animal life cycle, meiosis — a diploid cell undergoes meiosis using ATP resulting in a gamete in a testis. Here, the gamete is a daughter cell.
  • In alternation of generations, meiosis — a diploid cell undergoes meiosis using ATP resulting in a gamete in a sporophyte. Here, the gamete is a daughter cell.

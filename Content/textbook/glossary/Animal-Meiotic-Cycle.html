Animal meiotic cycle

animal meiotic cycle

An ordered sequence of events in which a parent sex cell divides to make 4 daughter cells which are gametes. The cell cycle includes interphase followed by meoisis (inclusive of cytokinesis).

Definition of an animal meiotic cycle

An ordered sequence of events in which a parent sex cell divides to make 4 daughter cells which are gametes. The cell cycle includes interphase followed by meoisis (inclusive of cytokinesis).

Animal meiotic cycle is a type of: meiotic cell cycle .

Signal transduction

signal transduction

A stage in cell signaling in which the signal is converted to a form that can bring about a specific cellular response. It can be said to be the linkage of a mechanical, chemical, or electromagnetic stimulus to a specific cellular response.

Definition of a signal transduction

A stage in cell signaling in which the signal is converted to a form that can bring about a specific cellular response. It can be said to be the linkage of a mechanical, chemical, or electromagnetic stimulus to a specific cellular response.

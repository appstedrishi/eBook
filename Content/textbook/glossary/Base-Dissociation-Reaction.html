Properties of a base dissociation reaction

  • Free energy change
    • inherent to all exergonic reactions
      • negative with respect to an endergonic reaction

Participants of a base dissociation reaction

Base dissociation reaction consumes a base compound and produces a cation and a hydroxide ion. This process releases free-energy.

Steps of base dissociation reaction

  • Reverse reaction consumes a chemical and produces a base compound and another chemical
  • Forward reaction consumes 2 base compounds and produces a chemical

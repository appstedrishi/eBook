Functions of a receptor protein

  • Signal reception — a cell receives a signal at a receptor protein carried by a chemical
  • In signal transduction with scaffolding protein, attach — a scaffolding protein is attached to a receptor protein at a receptor site

Some functions of different types of receptor proteins

When acetylcholine molecules (ACh) bind to two of the subunits, the gate opens and Na + flows into the cell. This channel helps regulate membrane polarity.

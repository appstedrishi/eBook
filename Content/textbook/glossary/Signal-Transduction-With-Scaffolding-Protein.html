Signal transduction with scaffolding protein

signal transduction with scaffolding protein

Signal transduction event in which a receptor is attached to a protein that scaffolds or provides structural integrity to the cell. These scaffolding proteins act as relay proteins during cell signaling to increase efficiency of signal transduction.

Definition of a signal transduction with scaffolding protein

Steps of signal transduction with scaffolding protein

  • A receptor protein is activated resulting in another receptor protein
  • A scaffolding protein is attached to a receptor protein at a receptor site
  • A relay protein is attached to a scaffolding protein
  • A relay protein is activated resulting in a protein kinase in a scaffolding protein
  • A chemical is activated by a scaffolding protein

Properties of a storage polysaccharide breakdown

  • Free energy change
    • inherent to all exergonic reactions
      • negative with respect to an endergonic reaction

Participants of a storage polysaccharide breakdown

Break down in a cell using a storage polysaccharide resulting in at least 2 monomers and at least 2 monosaccharides. This process transforms activation energy to free-energy.

Types of storage polysaccharide breakdowns

  • hydrolysis of starch
  • glycogen breakdown

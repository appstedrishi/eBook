Definition of a hypertonic solution

A hypertonic solution is one with a high concentration of solutes when compared to another solution which is separated from it by a semipermeable membrane. As opposed to an isotonic solution or a hypotonic solution.

Hypertonic solution is a type of: aqueous solution.

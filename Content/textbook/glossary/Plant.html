Functions of a plant

  • carbon dioxide is converted by a plant in a plant cell to glucose and an oxygen molecule. This process transforms free-energy and visible light to chemical energy.
  • starch is stored by a plant
  • In seedling, starch is stored by a plant in a root

A plant also does…

  • Nutrition — a plant obtains nutrition using carbon dioxide and visible light resulting in a chemical. Here, the plant is a photoautotroph.

Functions of an RNA polymerase

  • Synthesis of an RNA and a water molecule by a transcription factor in a cell and a transcription unit toward a 3 prime end in another cell using a gene from a 5 prime end using a molecule and a monomer in an active site. This process transforms activation energy and free-energy to another free-energy.
  • Synthesis of mRNA — an MRNA and a water molecule are synthesized in a cell using a molecule and a monomer. This process transforms activation energy and free-energy to another free-energy.
  • Synthesis of RNA In eukaryote — an RNA and a water molecule are synthesized in a eukaryotic cell using 2 monomers in a nucleus. This process transforms activation energy and free-energy to another free-energy.

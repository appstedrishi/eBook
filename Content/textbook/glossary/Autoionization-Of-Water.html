autoionization of water

Autoionization of water is the reversible reaction during which water dissociates forming hydroxide (OH-) and hydronium (H3O+) ions and reforms water from these same ions at an equal rate.

Definition of an autoionization of water

Autoionization of water is the reversible reaction during which water dissociates forming hydroxide (OH-) and hydronium (H3O+) ions and reforms water from these same ions at an equal rate.

Autoionization of water is a type of: chemical equilibrium reaction .

One gene-one polypeptide

one gene-one polypeptide

One Gene One Polypeptide hypothesis is concerned with the hypothesis that a gene has a function of dictating the synthesis of a specific Polypeptide. This hypothesis was a restatement of Beadle and Tatum's one gene one enzyme hypothesis. This hypothesis was based on the fact that many enzymes being protein are constructed from two or more different polypeptide chains

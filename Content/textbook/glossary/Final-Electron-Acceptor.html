Definition of a final electron acceptor

The molecule which accepts the electron at the end of an electron transport chain.

Final electron acceptor is a type of: electron recipient .

Examples of a final electron acceptor

  • Reduction — an electron transfers by a cyanobacterium from a chemical to an iron. Here, the iron is a final electron acceptor.
  • Reduction — an electron transfers from a chemical to an oxygen molecule. Here, the oxygen molecule is a final electron acceptor.

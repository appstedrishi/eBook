Hybrid orbital

hybrid orbital

A type of orbital that forms as a result of mixing different kinds of atomic orbitals on the same atom. For example, mixing one s orbital and 3 p orbitals to form an sp3 hybrid orbital.

Definition of a hybrid orbital

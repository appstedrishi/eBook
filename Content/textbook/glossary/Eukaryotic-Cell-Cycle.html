Eukaryotic cell cycle

eukaryotic cell cycle

An ordered sequence of events in the life of a cell, from its origin in the division of a parent cell until its own division into two. The eukaryotic cell cycle is composed of interphase, (including G1, S & G2 subphases) and M phase (including mitosis and cytokinesis).

Definition of a eukaryotic cell cycle

An ordered sequence of events in the life of a cell, from its origin in the division of a parent cell until its own division into two. The eukaryotic cell cycle is composed of interphase, (including G1, S & G2 subphases) and M phase (including mitosis and cytokinesis).

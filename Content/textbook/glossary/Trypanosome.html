Definition of a trypanosome

a genus of kinetoplastids, a monophyletic group of unicellular parasitic flagellate protozoa. All trypanosomes are heteroxenous (requiring more than one obligatory host to complete life cycle) and are transmitted via a vector.

Trypanosome is a type of: kinetoplastid.

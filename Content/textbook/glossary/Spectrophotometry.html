Spectrophotometry

spectrophotometry

The study of light absorption by using the instrument, spectrophotometer. Absorption occurs when energy from the radiative source is absorbed by the material. Absorption is often determined by measuring the fraction of energy transmitted through the material; absorption will decrease the transmitted portion.

Participants of a spectrophotometry

Spectrophotometry — a scientist measures absorption by a molecule using a spectrophotometer resulting in an absorption spectrum. This process requires light.

Blebbing

blebbing

During apoptosis, a process in which the cytoskeleton detaches from the plasma membrane, creating bulges in the cell called blebs. These blebs will later rupture as the cell dies.

Definition of a blebbing

During apoptosis, a process in which the cytoskeleton detaches from the plasma membrane, creating bulges in the cell called blebs. These blebs will later rupture as the cell dies.

Blebbing is a type of: cellular process.

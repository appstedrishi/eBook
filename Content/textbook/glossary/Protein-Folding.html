protein folding

The process of altering the shape of a polypeptide through interactions such as hydrogen bonds, hydrophobic interactions, and disulfide bridges to create a functional protein.

Definition of a protein folding

The process of altering the shape of a polypeptide through interactions such as hydrogen bonds, hydrophobic interactions, and disulfide bridges to create a functional protein.

Protein folding is a type of: biomolecular process .

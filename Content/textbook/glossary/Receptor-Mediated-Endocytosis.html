Participants of a receptor mediated endocytosis

Transport of a chemical by means of a cell surface receptor in a eukaryotic cell to cytoplasm inside a coated vesicle from an extracellular side through a plasma membrane using an organic molecule. This process requires chemical energy and free-energy.

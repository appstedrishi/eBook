Steps of alternation of generations

  1. Meiosis — a diploid cell undergoes meiosis using ATP resulting in 2 cells (a gamete) in a sporophyte
  2. Create produces a gametophyte
  3. Mitosis — a nucleus undergoes mitosis in a eukaryotic cell resulting in 2 nuclei in a gametophyte
  4. Fertilization — a gamete is fertilized in another gamete resulting in a zygote
  5. Create produces a sporophyte

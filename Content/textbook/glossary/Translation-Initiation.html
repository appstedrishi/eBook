Participants of a translation initiation

Translation initiation — a GTP and an initiation factor initiate translation in a cell resulting in a translation initiation complex in a ribosome. This process transforms free-energy to thermal energy.

Steps of translation initiation

  • A GTP is broken resulting in a GDP and a phosphate ion
  • Synthesis reaction — polypeptide is synthesized from a chemical at an amino end
  • A five prime cap, an initiation factor and poly(A) tail attach to an MRNA using a GTP at a leader segment
  • initiator tRNA is attached to an AUG using a GTP at a P-Site

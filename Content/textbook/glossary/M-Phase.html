Definition of an M-phase

The phase of the cell cycle that includes mitosis and cytokinesis.

M-phase is a type of: cell division.

Steps of M-phase

  1. Mitosis — a nucleus undergoes mitosis in a eukaryotic cell resulting in 2 nuclei
  2. Cytokinesis — cytoplasm undergoes cytokinesis in a eukaryotic cell producing 2 eukaryotic cells

Definition of a beta 1,4 linkage

A chemical bond between the number 1 and a number 4 carbon on two adjacent molecules where the oxygen atom from the hydroxyl group of the monosaccharide is above the plane of the molecule.

Beta 1,4 linkage is a type of: glycosidic linkage .

Figure 9.7 The Oxidation of NADH and FADH2 in the Respiratory

Electrons from NADH and FADH2 are passed along the respiratory chain, a series of protein complexes in the inner mitochondrial membrane containing electron carriers and enzymes. The carriers gain free energy when they become reduced and release free energy when they are oxidized. This illustration shows the standard free energy changes along the respiratory chain.

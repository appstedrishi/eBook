Figure 11.21 Apoptosis: Programmed Cell Death

(A) Many cells are programmed to “self-destruct” when they are no longer needed, or when they have lived long enough to accumulate a burden of DNA damage that might harm the organism. (B) Both external and internal signals stimulate caspases, the enzymes that break down specific cell constituents, resulting in apoptosis.

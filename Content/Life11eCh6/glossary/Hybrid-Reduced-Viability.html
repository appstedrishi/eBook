Hybrid reduced viability

hybrid reduced viability

A post zygotic reproctive barrier resulting from genetic incompatibility between the two species. Reduced hybrid viability may result in abortion of development or a frail organism.

Definition of a hybrid reduced viability

A post zygotic reproctive barrier resulting from genetic incompatibility between the two species. Reduced hybrid viability may result in abortion of development or a frail organism.

Hybrid reduced viability is a type of: reproductive isolation .

Solubility

solubility

Property indicating the maximum amount of solute that can dissolve per amount of solvent under the specified conditions (when a value is given), or the ability of a solute to be dissolved in a solvent. In biology, it is popular to indicate that a molecule is soluble in water or soluble in lipid.

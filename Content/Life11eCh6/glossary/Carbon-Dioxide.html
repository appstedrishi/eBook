Definition of carbon dioxide

Carbon dioxide (chemical formula CO2) is an inorganic chemical compound composed of two oxygen atoms covalently bonded to a single carbon atom.

Carbon dioxide is a type of: covalent compound , nonpolar molecule , and inorganic molecule .

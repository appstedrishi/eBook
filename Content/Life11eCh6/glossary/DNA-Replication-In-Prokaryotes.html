Participants of a DNA replication In prokaryotes

A DNA (a template) is copied by a DNA ligase, a DNA polymerase III and primase in a prokaryote to a 5 prime end from a 3 prime end using 2 monomers resulting in another DNA and a water molecule in cytoplasm. This process transforms activation energy and free-energy to another free-energy.

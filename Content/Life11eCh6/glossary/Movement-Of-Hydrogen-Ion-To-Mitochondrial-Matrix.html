Movement of hydrogen ion to mitochondrial matrix

movement of hydrogen ion to mitochondrial matrix

The passive transfer of hydrogen ions, driven by the proton-motive force, from the intermembrane space to the mitochondrial matrix via ATP synthase, a protein embedded in the inner mitochondrial membrane. The diffusion of H+ ions through ATP synthase drives the synthesis of ATP in the mitochondria.

cell cell recognition

Process in which a cell recognizes another cell through direct contact between outer cell structures that initiates a signal transduction event resulting in recognition of the cell being perceived.

Definition of a cell cell recognition

Process in which a cell recognizes another cell through direct contact between outer cell structures that initiates a signal transduction event resulting in recognition of the cell being perceived.

Cell cell recognition is a type of: intercellular process .

Bluefin Tuna Do Not Recognize Boundaries (A) Marine biologist Barbara Block attaches computerized data-recording tracking tags to a live bluefin tuna before returning it to the Atlantic Ocean, where its travels will be monitored. Participants of a cell cell recognition

A cell recognizes another cell (a target cell) at a plasma membrane

Steps of cell cell recognition

  • A cell makes contact with another cell
  • A cell is differentiated by another cell

Definition of a foot

The portion of a bryophyte sporophyte that gathers sugars, amino acids, water, and minerals from the parent gametophyte via transfer cells. (2) One of the three main parts of a mollusc; a muscular structure usually used for movement. See also mantle, visceral mass. (3) In vertebrates, the terminal portion of a limb which bears weight and allows locomotion

Foot is a type of: specialised organ .

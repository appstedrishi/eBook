Participants of a synthesis of lysosome

A lysosome is synthesized by an endomembranous system in a eukaryotic cell using an organic molecule

Steps of synthesis of lysosome

  • Synthesis of membrane protein — a hydrolase, membrane protein and a water molecule are synthesized in a eukaryotic cell using 2 monomers in a rough endoplasmic reticulum. This process transforms activation energy and free-energy to another free-energy.
  • Chemical reaction — a hydrolase is converted to another hydrolase in Golgi apparatus
  • A vesicle is removed from Golgi apparatus in a trans face
  • A vesicle is fused to a lysosome

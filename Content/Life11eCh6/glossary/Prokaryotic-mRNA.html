prokaryotic mRNA

An RNA transcript, often of an operon, a cluster of genes, in the cytoplasm of a prokaryote, such as bacteria. Prokaryotic mRNA is translated directly into protein without undergoing any post-transcriptional processing as in the eukaryotic cell.

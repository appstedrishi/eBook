Definition of a host

The larger participant in a symbiotic relationship, serving as home and food source for the smaller symbiont.

Examples of a host

  • Attach — phage DNA is attached to a prokaryotic chromosome. Here, a prokaryote is a host.
  • DNA transfer — a DNA piece and a proto-oncogene are transferred to a cell to a prokaryote resulting in cancer, an oncogene and recombinants. Here, another cell is a host.
  • A cell is a host in the replication of a genome
  • A cell is infected by an infectious particle. Here, the cell is a host.
  • A cell is infected by an HIV and a virus. Here, the cell is a host.

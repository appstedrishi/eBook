Translocation

translocation

(1) A rearrangement of parts between nonhomologous chromosomes. (2) In protein synthesis, the third stage in the construction of a growing polypeptide chain, when the RNA carrying the polypeptide chain moves from the A spot to the P spot on the ribosome. (3) The movement of organic materials in the phloem of vascular plants.

Definition of a translocation

(1) A rearrangement of parts between nonhomologous chromosomes. (2) In protein synthesis, the third stage in the construction of a growing polypeptide chain, when the RNA carrying the polypeptide chain moves from the A spot to the P spot on the ribosome. (3) The movement of organic materials in the phloem of vascular plants.

Definition of a lysosomal enzyme

Lysosomal enzymes are synthesized in the cytosol and the endoplasmic reticulum. Some important lysosomal enzymes include lipase, carbohydrase, proteases, nucleases and phosphoric acid

Lysosomal enzyme is a type of: hydrolase.

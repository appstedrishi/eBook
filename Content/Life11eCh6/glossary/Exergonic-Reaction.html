Definition of an exergonic reaction

A spontaneous chemical reaction, in which there is a net release of free energy.

Exergonic reaction is a type of: exergonic process and energy reaction.

Properties of an exergonic reaction

  • Free energy change
    • negative with respect to an endergonic reaction

Participants of an exergonic reaction

Exergonic reaction consumes a chemical and produces another chemical. This process releases free-energy.

Types of exergonic reactions

  • hydrolysis
  • electron transport chain reaction
  • combustion reaction
  • chemical dissociation reaction
  • cellular respiration of complex carbohydrate
  • catabolic pathway
  • bioluminescence

Functions of a mammalian cell

  • 2 hydrogen ions are transported by an ATP synthase and a carrier protein to a hypotonic solution from a hypertonic solution through an inner membrane using an organic molecule resulting in a hydrogen ion gradient. This process transforms chemical energy and free-energy to a proton-motive force.

A mammalian cell also does…

  • In M-phase during human mitotic cycle, cytoplasm undergoes cytokinesis in a human cell producing another human cell and a mammalian cell. Here, the mammalian cell is a daughter cell.
  • In human mitotic cycle, M-phase consumes ATP and produces a human cell and a mammalian cell. Here, the mammalian cell is a daughter cell.
  • A digestive enzyme is secreted by a mammalian cell. Here, the mammalian cell is a secretor.

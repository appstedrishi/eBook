Electron carrier of cellular respiration

electron carrier of cellular respiration

A coenzyme that accepts electrons stripped from glucose at various steps of cellular respiration, then gives them up in the electron transport chain. These coenzymes include NAD+ and FAD+, which become NADH and FADH2 when they carry electrons.

Definition of an electron carrier of cellular respiration

A coenzyme that accepts electrons stripped from glucose at various steps of cellular respiration, then gives them up in the electron transport chain. These coenzymes include NAD+ and FAD+, which become NADH and FADH2 when they carry electrons.

Electron carrier of cellular respiration is a type of: electron carrier.

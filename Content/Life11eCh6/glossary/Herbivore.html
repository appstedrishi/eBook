Definition of a herbivore

An animal that mainly eats plants or algae.

Herbivore is a type of: heterotroph.

Examples of a herbivore

  • Nutrition — a termite obtains nutrition from algae using 2 carbons, an organic molecule, a plant and a wood resulting in protein. Here, the termite is a herbivore.
  • Nutrition — a sea slug obtains nutrition from algae using 2 carbons, an organic molecule and a plant resulting in a carbohydrate and protein. Here, the sea slug is a herbivore.

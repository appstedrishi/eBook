Participants of a diffusion of anion across plasma membrane

Diffusion of an anion and a hydrophilic compound by means of a concentration gradient to a hypotonic solution from cytoplasm with a transport protein from a hypertonic solution through a plasma membrane using a membrane potential to an extra cellular matrix. This process transforms potential energy to free-energy.

Steps of diffusion of anion across plasma membrane

  • An anion, a hydrophilic compound and an oxygen molecule are moved by a transport protein through a plasma membrane to a hypotonic solution from a hypertonic solution through a cytoplasmic side and a hydrophobic core

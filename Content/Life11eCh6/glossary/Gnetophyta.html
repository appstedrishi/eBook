Functions of a gnetophyta

  • carbon dioxide and a water molecule are converted by a gnetophyta in a plant cell to glucose and an oxygen molecule at cytoplasm. This process transforms free-energy and visible light to chemical energy.
  • starch is stored by a gnetophyta in a seed and a taproot

Spore

spore

A haploid cell produced in the sporophyte by meiosis in the life cycle of a plant or alga undergoing alternation of generations. A spore can divide by mitosis to develop into a multicellular haploid individual, the gametophyte, without fusing with another cell. (2) In fungi, a haploid cell, produced either sexually or asexually, that produces a mycelium after germination.

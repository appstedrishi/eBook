Definition of an anaphase

The spindle fibres shorten and the centromere splits, separated sister chromatids are pulled along behind the centromeres. In the cytoplasm, the mitotic spindle begins to form. it is made of microtubules extending from the two centrosomes

Anaphase is a type of: mitotic stage.

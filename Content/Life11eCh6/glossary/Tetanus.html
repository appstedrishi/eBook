tetanus

The maximal, sustained contraction of a skeletal muscle, caused by a very high frequency of action potentials elicited by continual stimulation. Caused by clostridium tetani through wound infection

Definition of tetanus

The maximal, sustained contraction of a skeletal muscle, caused by a very high frequency of action potentials elicited by continual stimulation. Caused by clostridium tetani through wound infection

Tetanus is a type of: bacterial disease .

Definition of a facilitated diffusion of ion

Facilitated diffusion of ion is a type of: passive transport of solute and facilitated diffusion .

Properties of a facilitated diffusion of ion

  • Free energy change
    • inherent to all spontaneous changes
      • negative with respect to a non spontaneous change

Participants of a facilitated diffusion of ion

Diffusion by an ion by means of a concentration gradient to a hypotonic solution with a transport protein from a hypertonic solution through a biomembrane using a membrane potential. This process transforms potential energy to free-energy.

Steps of facilitated diffusion of ion

  • An ion is moved by a transport protein through a biomembrane through a hydrophobic core

Types of facilitated diffusion of ions

  • release of calcium ion by endoplasmic reticulum
  • facilitated diffusion of hydrated ions
  • diffusion of cation across plasma membrane
  • diffusion of anion across plasma membrane

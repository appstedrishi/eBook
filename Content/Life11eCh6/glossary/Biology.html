Medical Applications of Biology Improve Human Health Vaccination to prevent disease is a biologically based medical practice that began in the eighteenth century. Today evolutionary biology and genomics provide the basis for constant updates to vaccines that protect humans from virus-borne diseases such as flu and measles. In the developed world, vaccinations are so commonplace that some are offered on a drive-through basis.

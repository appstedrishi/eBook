Definition of a floral meristem

The shoot apical meristem is transformed into an inflorescence meristem, which goes on to produce the floral meristem, which produces the familiar sepals, petals, stamens, and carpels of the flower during the developmental process of flowering.

Floral meristem is a type of: meristem.

Feedback inhibition of glycolysis by ATP

feedback inhibition of glycolysis by ATP

A method of metabolic control of glycolysis in which excess ATP molecules produced during glycolysis act as an inhibitor of phosphofructokinase (PFK), an enzyme within the glycolytic pathway.

Definition of a feedback inhibition of glycolysis by ATP

A method of metabolic control of glycolysis in which excess ATP molecules produced during glycolysis act as an inhibitor of phosphofructokinase (PFK), an enzyme within the glycolytic pathway.

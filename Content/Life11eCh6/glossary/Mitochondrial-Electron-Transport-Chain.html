Mitochondrial electron transport chain

mitochondrial electron transport chain

A sequence of electron carrier molecules (membrane proteins) that shuttle electrons during the redox reactions that release energy used to make ATP. The electron transport chain assembly is present in the inner membrane of mitochondria of eukaryotes.

Definition of a mitochondrial electron transport chain

A sequence of electron carrier molecules (membrane proteins) that shuttle electrons during the redox reactions that release energy used to make ATP. The electron transport chain assembly is present in the inner membrane of mitochondria of eukaryotes.

Mitochondrial electron transport chain is a type of: electron transport chain .

Definition of a hypotonic solution

A hypotonic solution is one with a low concentration of solutes when compared to another solution which is separated from it by a semipermeable membrane. As opposed to an isotonic solution or a hypertonic solution.

Hypotonic solution is a type of: aqueous solution.

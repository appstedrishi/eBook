  • Free energy change
    • inherent to all exergonic reactions

Participants of a chemical reaction between methane and oxygen

Chemical reaction between methane and oxygen consumes a methane and an oxygen molecule and produces carbon dioxide and a water molecule. This process releases free-energy.

Steps of chemical reaction between methane and oxygen

  • Reduction — an electron transfers from a methane to an oxygen molecule. This process requires activation energy.
  • Produce produces carbon dioxide
  • A move
  • A lose
  • Oxidation — an electron transfers from a methane to an oxygen molecule. This process requires activation energy.

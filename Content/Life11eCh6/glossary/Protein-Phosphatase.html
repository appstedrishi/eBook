Definition of a protein phosphatase

An enzyme that cleaves phosphate groups from other proteins. Protein phosphatases often work to reverse the activity of protein kinases.

Functions of a protein phosphatase

  • Dephosphorylation — protein is converted to an anion and another protein in an active site. This process requires energy.

A protein phosphatase also does…

  • Dephosphorylation — energy and protein are converted to an anion and another protein in an active site. Here, a protein phosphatase is a catalyst.

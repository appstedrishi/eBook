E-Site

The third of three RNA binding sites on a ribisome that binds a tRNA molecule that has just transferred its amino acid to the growing polypeptide chain before it exits the ribisome.

Definition of an E-Site

The third of three RNA binding sites on a ribisome that binds a tRNA molecule that has just transferred its amino acid to the growing polypeptide chain before it exits the ribisome.

E-Site is a type of: ribosome region.

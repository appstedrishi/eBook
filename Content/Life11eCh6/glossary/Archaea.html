Definition of an archaea

Prokaryote that is classified in the Archaea domain, one of two prokaryotic domains for Prokaryotes. The other domain is Bacteria.

Archaea is a type of: prokaryote.

The aqua branches within Eukarya represent various groups of microbial eukaryotes, more commonly known as protists. The organisms on any one branch share a common ancestor.

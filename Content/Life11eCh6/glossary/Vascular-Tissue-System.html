vascular tissue system

Definition of a vascular tissue system

Vascular tissue system connects leaves and roots, allowing sugars to move from leaves to roots in the phloem and allowing water and minerals to move to the leaves in the xylem.

Vascular tissue system is a type of: tissue system.

Definition of an enzyme inhibitor

Enzyme inhibitors are molecules that bind to enzymes and decrease their activity.

Enzyme inhibitor is a type of: inhibitor and enzyme regulator.

Examples of an enzyme inhibitor

  • The function of antimicrobial protein is inhibition of a protein enzyme by an antimicrobial protein. Here, the antimicrobial protein is an enzyme inhibitor.
  • The function of ampicillin is inhibition of a protein enzyme by an ampicillin. Here, the ampicillin is an enzyme inhibitor.
  • The function of DDT is inhibition of a protein enzyme by a DDT. Here, the DDT is an enzyme inhibitor.
  • A protein enzyme is inhibit by antibiotic. Here, the antibiotic is an enzyme inhibitor.

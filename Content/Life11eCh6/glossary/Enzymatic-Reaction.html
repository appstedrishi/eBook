enzymatic reaction

Reactions which are catalyzed by enzymes. Most metabolic reactions are reversible, and an enzyme can catalyze either the forward or the reverse reaction, depending on which direction has a negative deltaG.

Definition of an enzymatic reaction

Reactions which are catalyzed by enzymes. Most metabolic reactions are reversible, and an enzyme can catalyze either the forward or the reverse reaction, depending on which direction has a negative deltaG.

Enzymatic reaction is a type of: chemical reaction .

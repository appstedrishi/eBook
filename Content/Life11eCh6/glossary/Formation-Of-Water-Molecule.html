Participants of a formation of water molecule

Formation of water molecule consumes a hydroxide ion and 2 molecules and produces a water molecule. This process transforms free-energy to another free-energy.

Steps of formation of water molecule

  • A hydrogen ion is added to a hydroxide ion to produce a water molecule

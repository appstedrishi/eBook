Participants of a cholera process

Cholera process — Vibrio cholerae causes disease in a person

Steps of cholera process

  1. Bacterial biofilm formation — Vibrio cholerae forms a bacterial biofilm in a small intestine at a lining
  2. A cholera toxin is produced by Vibrio cholerae
  3. Synthesis of cAMP — ATP and a molecule are converted in an animal cell to cyclic AMP and pyrophosphate. This process transforms free-energy to another free-energy.
  4. Secretion — an animal cell secretes salt
  5. Osmosis — a water molecule diffuses by means of a concentration gradient to a hypertonic solution from a hypotonic solution through an animal plasma membrane. This process transforms potential energy to free-energy.

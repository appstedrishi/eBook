Movement of endomembranous system membrane of a biomembrane

Steps of movement of endomembranous system membrane

  1. A biomembrane is moved to an endoplasmic reticulum from a nuclear membrane
  2. A biomembrane is moved to Golgi apparatus from an endoplasmic reticulum
  3. A biomembrane is moved to a plasma membrane from Golgi apparatus

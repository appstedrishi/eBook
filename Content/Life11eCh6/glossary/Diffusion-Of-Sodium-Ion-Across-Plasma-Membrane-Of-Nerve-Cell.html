Participants of a diffusion of sodium ion across plasma membrane of nerve cell

Diffusion of a hydrophilic compound and a sodium ion by means of a concentration gradient to a hypotonic solution with a sodium gated ion-channel from a hypertonic solution through an animal plasma membrane using a membrane potential. This process transforms potential energy to free-energy.

Steps of diffusion of sodium ion across plasma membrane of nerve cell

Functions of an electrogenic pump

  • A concentration gradient is maintained by an electrogenic pump
  • An ion is transported by an electrogenic pump to a solution from another solution through a biomembrane using an organic molecule. This process requires chemical energy and free-energy.
  • Facilitated diffusion — a chemical diffuses to an aqueous solution using an electrogenic pump from another aqueous solution through a biomembrane. This process transforms potential energy to free-energy.

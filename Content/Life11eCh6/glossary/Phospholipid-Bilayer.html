phospholipid bilayer

An arrangement of cellular membranes in which phospholipid tails face each other due to hydrophobic properties resulting in 2 layers of phospholipids.

Definition of a phospholipid bilayer

An arrangement of cellular membranes in which phospholipid tails face each other due to hydrophobic properties resulting in 2 layers of phospholipids.

Phospholipid bilayer is a type of: cellular structure .

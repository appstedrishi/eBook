Iron-sulfur protein

iron-sulfur protein

Proteins with clusters of non-heme iron, cysteine sulfur, and usually inorganic sulfur that function as electron-transfer groups in most iron-sulfur proteins, but in other proteins they have functions, such as catalysis of hydratase and dehydratase reactions, maintaining protein structure, or regulating activity.

Sodium-potassium pump

sodium-potassium pump

An transport protein that actively transports three sodium ions out of the cell for every two potassium ions it pumps into the cell.

Definition of a sodium-potassium pump

An transport protein that actively transports three sodium ions out of the cell for every two potassium ions it pumps into the cell.

Sodium-potassium pump is a type of: carrier protein and electrogenic pump .

AUG

A triplet of nucleotides present on the mRNA that codes for the amino acid, methionine, serves as the start or initiation codon for protein synthesis, and is the place where the first tRNA binds to the mRNA during translation.

Definition of an AUG

A triplet of nucleotides present on the mRNA that codes for the amino acid, methionine, serves as the start or initiation codon for protein synthesis, and is the place where the first tRNA binds to the mRNA during translation.

AUG is a type of: start codon.

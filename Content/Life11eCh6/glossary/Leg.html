A leg is an anatomical animal structure used for locomotion. The distal end is often modified to distribute force (such as a foot). Most animals have an even number of legs.

Definition of a leg

A leg is an anatomical animal structure used for locomotion. The distal end is often modified to distribute force (such as a foot). Most animals have an even number of legs.

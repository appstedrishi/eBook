Adaptive immunity

adaptive immunity

A vertebrate-specific defense that is mediated by B lymphocytes (B Cells) and T lymphocytes (T cells). It exhibits specificity, memory, and self-nonself recognition.

Definition of an adaptive immunity

A vertebrate-specific defense that is mediated by B lymphocytes (B Cells) and T lymphocytes (T cells). It exhibits specificity, memory, and self-nonself recognition.

Functions of a chief cell

  • pepsinogen and thermal energy are released by a chief cell to a solution from an aqueous solution

A chief cell also does…

  • pepsinogen and polypeptide are secreted by a chief cell to an extracellular side with a transport vesicle from cytoplasm through an animal plasma membrane using chemical energy, free-energy and an organic molecule. Here, the chief cell is a secretor.

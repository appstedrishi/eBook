Participants of a mismatch repair

A damaged DNA strand is repaired by a DNA polymerase, protein and a repair enzyme in a DNA in a cell

Steps of mismatch repair

  • A damaged DNA strand is recognized by a repair enzyme
  • A deoxyribonucleoside monophosphate and a mismatched nucleotide are replaced in a damaged DNA strand

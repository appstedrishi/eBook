Participants of a translocation of phloem sap

Translocation of phloem sap — a phloem sap is translocated to a plant organ from another plant organ

Steps of translocation of phloem sap

  1. Loading of sugar into sieve tube element — sugar is loaded to a sieve-tube element from a mesophyll cell
  2. Osmosis — water and a water molecule diffuse by means of a concentration gradient to a hypertonic solution from a hypotonic solution through a biomembrane. This process transforms potential energy to free-energy.
  3. Pressure flow — a phloem sap is pressured flow to a plant organ from another plant organ through a sieve tube
  4. Unloading of sugar At sugar sink — a sugar substance is unloaded to a plant organ from a sieve tube

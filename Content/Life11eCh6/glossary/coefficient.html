Coefficient

coefficient

a quantity (usually a numerical constant) which is multiplied by another quantity following it in an expression; e.g. in the algebraic expression 4 x 2 - 6 x, the coefficient of (the first term) x 2 is 4 and the coefficient of (the second term) x is -6.

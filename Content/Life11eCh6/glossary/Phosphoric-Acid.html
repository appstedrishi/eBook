Definition of phosphoric acid

A mineral (inorganic) acid having the chemical formula H3PO4. Phosphoric acid molecules can combine with themselves to form a variety of compounds which are also referred to as phosphoric acids, but in a more general way.

Phosphoric acid is a type of: inorganic acid.

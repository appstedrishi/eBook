Definition of C4 plant

Functions of C4 plant

  • C4-photosynthesis — C4 plant performs C3-photosynthesis in a plant cell using carbon dioxide and a water molecule resulting in glucose and an oxygen molecule in a chloroplast and cytoplasm. This process transforms free-energy and visible light to chemical energy.
  • starch is stored by C4 plant in a root

C4 plant also does…

  • Nutrition — C4 plant obtains nutrition using carbon dioxide and visible light resulting in a chemical. Here, the C4 plant is a photoautotroph.

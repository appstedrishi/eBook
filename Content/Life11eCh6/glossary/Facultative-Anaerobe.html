Facultative anaerobe

facultative anaerobe

A cell or an organism that makes ATP by aerobic respiration if oxygen is present but that switches to anaerobic respiration or fermentation if oxygen is not present.

Definition of a facultative anaerobe

A cell or an organism that makes ATP by aerobic respiration if oxygen is present but that switches to anaerobic respiration or fermentation if oxygen is not present.

Facultative anaerobe is a type of: anaerobe.

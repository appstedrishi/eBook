Participants of a eukaryotic Calvin cycle

Eukaryotic Calvin cycle in a eukaryotic cell using ATP, carbon dioxide, an NADPH and ribulose bisphosphate resulting in an ADP, an NADP plus, a phosphate ion and sugar in an active site. This process requires free-energy.

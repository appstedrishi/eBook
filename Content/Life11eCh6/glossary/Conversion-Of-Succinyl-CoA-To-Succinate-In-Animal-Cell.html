conversion of succinyl CoA to succinate In animal cell

Intermediate step in the citric acid cycle taking place in many animal tissue cells, in which a phosphate group is transferred to a molecule of GDP, displacing CoA from Succinyl CoA to produce a molecule of GTP and a molecule of succinate.

bone formation

The process in which minerals such as calcium are added to tissues to form bone. Bone formation occurs during development, growth and repair of bone tissue.

Definition of a bone formation

The process in which minerals such as calcium are added to tissues to form bone. Bone formation occurs during development, growth and repair of bone tissue.

Bone formation is a type of: animal process.

contractile ring

Contractile ring is a ring structure made up of myosin and actin microfilaments which is formed at mid-region of the animal cell. It brings about cytokinesis (division of cytoplasm to result in two daughter cells)

Definition of a contractile ring

Contractile ring is a ring structure made up of myosin and actin microfilaments which is formed at mid-region of the animal cell. It brings about cytokinesis (division of cytoplasm to result in two daughter cells)

Contractile ring is a type of: animal cell structure .

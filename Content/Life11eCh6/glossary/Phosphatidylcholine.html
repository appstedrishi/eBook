Phosphatidylcholine

phosphatidylcholine

A phospholipid that is a major component of cellular membranes and functions in the transport of lipoproteins in tissues. It contains a choline group.

Definition of a phosphatidylcholine

A phospholipid that is a major component of cellular membranes and functions in the transport of lipoproteins in tissues. It contains a choline group.

Phosphatidylcholine is a type of: phospholipid.

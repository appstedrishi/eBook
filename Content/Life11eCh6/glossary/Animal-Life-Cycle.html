Animal life cycle

animal life cycle

The sequence of developmental stages an animal undergoes from one generation to the next beginning with the fusion of male and female gametes, then growth to reproductive maturity, and finally the animal's production of gametes. The cycle begins again at this point, assuming the gametes are fertilized.

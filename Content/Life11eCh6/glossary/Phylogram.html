Phylogram

phylogram

a branching diagram or "tree" showing the inferred evolutionary relationships among various biological species or other entities based upon similarities and differences in their physical and/or genetic characteristics. It contains branch lengths proportional to the amount of genetic change.

Definition of a phylogram

a branching diagram or "tree" showing the inferred evolutionary relationships among various biological species or other entities based upon similarities and differences in their physical and/or genetic characteristics. It contains branch lengths proportional to the amount of genetic change.

Phylogram is a type of: phylogenetic tree .

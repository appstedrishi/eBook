cystinuria

Genetic disorder in which faulty transport proteins result in positively charged amino acids not being reabsorbed in the kidneys, leading to kidney stones.

Definition of a cystinuria

Genetic disorder in which faulty transport proteins result in positively charged amino acids not being reabsorbed in the kidneys, leading to kidney stones.

Cystinuria is a type of: inborn error of metabolism .

Immunization

immunization

The process of artificially generating resistance to a disease by introducing an inactive or weakened form of a pathogen to induce the production of memory cells as in active immunization or by administering antibodies specific for a particular microbe for immediate but temporary protection as in passive immunization.

Definition of an immunization

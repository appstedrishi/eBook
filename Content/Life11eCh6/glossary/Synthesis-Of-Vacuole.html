Participants of a synthesis of vacuole

A vacuole is synthesized by an endoplasmic reticulum and Golgi apparatus using an organic molecule. This process requires free-energy.

Steps of synthesis of vacuole

  • A vesicle is removed from Golgi apparatus in a trans face
  • A vesicle is fused to another vesicle resulting in a vacuole

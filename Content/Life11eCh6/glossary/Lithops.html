Functions of a lithops

  • Crassulacean acid metabolism — a lithops performs crassulacean acid metabolism in a mesophyll cell using carbon dioxide and a water molecule resulting in glucose and an oxygen molecule in a chloroplast and cytoplasm. This process transforms free-energy and visible light to chemical energy.

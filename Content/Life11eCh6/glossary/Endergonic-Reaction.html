Definition of an endergonic reaction

A nonspontaneous chemical reaction, in which free energy is absorbed from the surroundings.

Properties of an endergonic reaction

  • Free energy change
    • positive with respect to an exergonic reaction

Participants of an endergonic reaction

Endergonic reaction consumes a chemical and produces another chemical. This process requires free-energy.

Types of endergonic reactions

  • condensation reaction
  • anabolic pathway

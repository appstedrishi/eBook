Steps of reproductive cycle of enveloped virus

  • A virus leaves away from a eukaryotic cell
  • ATP, an amino acid, a protein enzyme and a TRNA are taken control of by an animal virus and a genome
  • A capsid, a genome, a glycoprotein and a phospholipid are joined at a plasma membrane resulting in a viral envelope and a virus
  • A capsid is removed by a protein enzyme from an animal virus
  • A genome is replicated by a protein enzyme resulting in another genome
  • Attach in a receptor protein
  • A viral envelope is attached to a plasma membrane
  • A capsid and a genome are moved by an animal virus in a plasma membrane to a eukaryotic cell through another plasma membrane

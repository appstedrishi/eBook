dephosphorylation of ATP

Definition of a dephosphorylation of ATP

The loss of one of three phosphate groups from a molecule of ATP. This allows the release of energy contained in the bond between the phosphate groups.

Dephosphorylation of ATP is a type of: hydrolysis and dephosphorylation .
